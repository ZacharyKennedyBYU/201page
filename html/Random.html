Why Labrador Retrievers are Awesome

  1. Their Fur Coat

    • Long enough to be fluffy
    • Not too long to make grooming impossible
    • Comes in 3 different colors

  2. Their Temperment

    • Energetic enough to be a good exercise partner
    • Gentle enough to be trusted with young children

  3. Their Ability to Learn

    • Can be easily taught many tricks
    • Intelligent enough to become service dogs
    • One of mine taught herself to open doors which is kind of crazy

  4. Their Swimming Ability

    • Have specialized webbed-paws and oily fur coat for swimming
    • Can entertain themselves with water for hours
    • Can fish things out of the pool if you ask them very nicely
